What is xAPI?

xAPI (Experience API) is the modern replacement for SCORM. Instead of just "completed yes/no" — it captures any learning experience as a simple actor + verb + object statement.

Examples:

  • • Madhu completed "Fire Safety Module 1"
  • • Priya scored 92% on "AML Assessment"
  • • Anil watched "Hand Hygiene Video"

Statements stream into an LRS (Learning Record Store), then flow into Power BI / SharePoint dashboards.
