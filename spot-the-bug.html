💡 Why these matter

  • Contrast: WCAG 1.4.3 — body text needs 4.5:1 against background
  • Alt text: WCAG 1.1.1 — non-decorative images need a description
  • Flashing: WCAG 2.3.1 — no flash >3× per second (epilepsy risk)
  • Time limits: WCAG 2.2.1 — adjustable, extendable, or absent
  • Click targets: WCAG 2.5.5 — minimum 44×44 pixels (mobile-friendly)
