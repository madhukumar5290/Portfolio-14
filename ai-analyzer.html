🎯 What we measure

  • Word & sentence count
  • Average sentence length
  • Flesch Reading Ease (0–100)
  • Flesch–Kincaid Grade Level

✓ Target zone

Workplace eLearning: Flesch 60–80, grade 7–9. Patient education: aim for grade 5–6.

Tips

How to improve your score

✂️

Shorten sentences

Aim for 15–20 words. Break compound sentences. Read aloud — if you run out of breath, it's too long.

🔤

Swap polysyllables

"utilise" → "use". "in order to" → "to". "approximately" → "about".

🎯

Lead with the verb

"You can submit the form by Friday" beats "Submission of the form should be completed by Friday."

👤

Talk to the learner

Use "you" and "we". The conversational register lifts every readability score.
