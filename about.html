About Me

Designer · Developer · Learning Storyteller

12+ years turning dry content into experiences people actually finish — and remember.

MK
Hi, I'm Madhu

From classroom teacher to learning engineer

I'm an instructional designer & eLearning developer based in Hyderabad, India — currently a Principal L&D SS Analyst, working with healthcare, banking, retail, education and manufacturing clients across India and the US.

My journey started in a Grade 8 classroom, where I learned the hardest truth of teaching: content alone never wins. What wins is empathy, clarity, and a story the learner can see themselves in.

Today I bring that same belief to every project — whether it's a 5-minute Rise microlearning, an Articulate Storyline scenario powered by 600 lines of custom JavaScript, or an AR experience built in ZapWorks.
